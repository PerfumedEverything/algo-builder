## Decisions Made
- **LAST_PRICE_DEALER over LAST_PRICE_EXCHANGE:** The T-Invest app shows dealer prices (includes algovist/market maker quotes). Exchange-only prices can lag by 0.25+ rub creating user-visible discrepancy.
- **Multi-board classCode list:** TQBR alone only covers shares. ETFs trade on TQTF, bonds on TQOB/TQCB. Having a broader list prevents wrong variant selection for non-share instruments like TGLD.
- **apiTradeAvailableFlag as tiebreaker:** When multiple variants exist with same classCode, the tradeable one is the active instrument.
